Know your why, some of the time

September 17, 2025

When pursuing long term goals, it's important to know and remember why you're pursuing those goals. This will keep you going after them even when there's resistance. But also important is to occasionally do things for no reason at all besides you felt like it in the moment.

These are 2 different muscles that are both important for a good life. Train all the muscles, some of the time.
